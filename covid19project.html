COVID-19 Project:

During the Spring Quarter of 2020, I was part of a class called IEMS 313: Optimization. In February, the professor of the course worked with the CDC to help optimize the distrobution of medical supplied in the United States to both minimize unmet demand and minimize total number of supplies used. During the quarter, we were given the same data and asked to create our own reports using what we learned about optimization.
